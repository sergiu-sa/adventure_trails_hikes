Hiking Lightly

Our eco promise: travel that heals the planet

Regenerative by Design

Every expedition is built to benefit both the environment and the people who protect it

Our hikes aren't just low-impact, they're net-positive. Every expedition is built to benefit both the environment and the people who protect it. We believe that tourism can be a force for good, creating positive change in every destination we visit.

Through innovative partnerships, cutting-edge technology, and deep community collaboration, we've created a model of adventure travel that leaves places better than we found them.

Our Sustainability Features

🌱 Zero Waste Kits

Reusable, compostable, and plastic-free gear for every hiker. Our proprietary kit eliminates 95% of single-use items.

♻️ 150% Carbon Offset

Beyond neutral. We invest in reforestation and renewable projects in each region, offsetting more carbon than we produce.

💧 Water Impact

Clean water systems funded through each booking. No bottled waste. Every hiker contributes to local water infrastructure.

🤝 Local Equity

75% of trip fees go directly to local guides, families, and conservation projects. Fair wages, fair trade, fair future.

🦋 Wildlife Protection

Conservation efforts, anti-poaching support, and habitat care. We fund wildlife corridors and protection programs.

🎒 Shared Gear Library

Community-led rental system of eco-certified gear. Reduce and reuse. High-quality equipment without the environmental cost.
