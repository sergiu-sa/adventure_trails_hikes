Our Mission

Creating transformative experiences that bridge cultures and protect our planet

We believe the most meaningful travel happens when challenge meets connection. Our mission is to guide hikers through the world's most breathtaking trails while fostering deep relationships with the people and cultures who inhabit them.

From day hikes to multi-week treks, we focus on cultural respect, sustainability, and unforgettable experiences rooted in the local way of life. Every journey with us supports local communities and conservation efforts.

What Makes Us Different

Community Partnerships

We work directly with local communities, ensuring your trip benefits the people who make it possible. 75% of our revenue stays local.

Eco-First Approach

From carbon offsets to low-impact gear, we lead the way in responsible hiking practices. Every trip is carbon negative.

Safety & Certification

Our guides are certified, experienced, and culturally sensitive, providing peace of mind and genuine insight into local traditions.

Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

Founded by Maria and David after a life-changing trek in Nepal. Started with just 3 local guides and a dream to revolutionize adventure travel.

2017

First International Expansion

Launched our Peru and Morocco programs. Established partnerships with indigenous communities and achieved B-Corp certification.

2019

Sustainability Leadership

Became the first hiking company to achieve carbon-negative status. Launched our community water project initiative.

2021

Digital Innovation

Developed virtual cultural workshops and launched our online learning platform to connect hikers with local communities year-round.

2023

Global Recognition

Awarded "Best Sustainable Tour Operator" by National Geographic. Expanded to 25 countries with 200+ local guide partners.
