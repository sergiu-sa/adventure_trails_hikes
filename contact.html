Frequently Asked Questions

All our trips include accommodation, meals, local transportation, certified guides, cultural activities, and sustainability contributions. International flights and personal gear are not included.

Fitness requirements vary by trip. We offer adventures from easy (suitable for most fitness levels) to extreme (requiring excellent physical condition). Each trip page lists specific requirements.

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for popular destinations. Some permits (like Everest Base Camp) require longer lead times. Last-minute bookings may be available but are limited.

Travel insurance is mandatory for all trips. We require coverage for medical expenses, evacuation, and trip cancellation. We can recommend trusted providers who specialize in adventure travel.
