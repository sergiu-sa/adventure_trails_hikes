02 / How an expedition runs.

From first contact to the last campfire — four phases, no improvisation.

  1. 01

    Plan

    Route briefing, gear list, permits, vaccinations, and a one-on-one fitness audit eight weeks out.

  2. 02

    Train

    Twelve-week conditioning plan, altitude acclimatization protocols, and weighted-pack progression.

  3. 03

    Hike

    Daily distances mapped to your conditioning curve. Local guide, satellite comms, evac insurance.

  4. 04

    Reflect

    Post-expedition debrief, photo deliverable, community impact report, and lifetime alumni access.

03 / Built for the people actually walking.

Three commitments that separate us from the brochure operators.

Certified guides who were born on the route.

Every expedition is co-led by a UIAGM-certified mountain guide and a local heritage guide. They speak the language, know the terrain in three weather systems, and have hosted thousands of hikers across six continents.

UIAGM Certified · Wilderness Medicine
Annapurna · 2024

Cultural immersion isn't a brochure line.

Meals are cooked by host families. Stories are told in the language they're meant to be told in. Seventy-five percent of trip revenue stays in the village. We don't visit communities — we're hosted by them.

Community Verified
Sacred Valley · 2023

Carbon negative since 2021.

Every trip is offset 150% via verified reforestation. Zero landfill kit, shared gear library, and direct community investment funded by your booking — not a checkbox at checkout. B-Corp certified, audited annually.

B-Corp · Leave No Trace
Patagonia · 2024
